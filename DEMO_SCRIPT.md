# Demo Video Script — QuickAI Short / Pre-Flight
# Target length: 2:50 – 3:00
# Record at: https://www.quickaishort.online (live, logged-in account)

---

## [0:00 – 0:15] Hook / Problem

**Say:**
"Every creator has posted a clip they thought would blow up — and it flopped.
The edit was fine. The hook felt strong. But they had no way to know how a real audience would react before posting.
This is Pre-Flight. Built on Google ADK. Let me show you."

**Screen:** quickaishort.online homepage, scrolling slowly over the feature cards.

---

## [0:15 – 0:45] Step 1 — Paste a YouTube URL

**Say:**
"I'll paste in a real YouTube video — a 20-minute tech talk. No prep, no mock data."

**Action:**
1. Navigate to `/editor`
2. Paste a real YouTube URL (e.g. a tech talk or podcast clip ~10–20 min)
3. Click Analyze

**Say:**
"The system sends audio to the backend — yt-dlp extracts it, Whisper transcribes it in the browser, then our viral heuristics score every 30-second window by audio energy and speech density."

**Screen:** Show the "Analyzing…" progress states in the left panel (transcribing → analyzing → done).

---

## [0:45 – 1:15] Step 2 — Clip Results

**Say:**
"We get ranked clips with viral scores, hooks, and reasoning — generated by Gemini 2.5 Flash."

**Action:**
1. Scroll through the clip list — show at least 3 clips with different scores
2. Click the highest-scoring clip
3. Show the video scrubbing to that timestamp

**Say:**
"Each clip shows why it works — the hook, the viral reasoning, the audience signal. Now here's where it gets interesting."

---

## [1:15 – 2:00] Step 3 — Pre-Flight Multi-Agent Pipeline

**Say:**
"Pre-Flight runs this clip through a Google ADK multi-agent system.
It starts with a validation DAG — checking clip quality, grounding predictions against trend data from Supabase."

**Action:** Click "Run Pre-Flight" on the selected clip (or navigate to the Pre-Flight panel).

**Say:**
"Then a LoopAgent spins up six AI personas in parallel on Gemini — Gen-Z, Millennials, Sports fans, Tech, Entertainment, News. Each one independently scores the clip for their demographic. An Aggregator synthesizes the results through a QualityGate, then a Refinement agent produces the final prediction."

**Screen:** Show the Pre-Flight panel animating through stages — DAG → personas → aggregation → result.

**Say:**
"The result: a cross-demographic confidence score and actionable notes on what to fix before posting."

**Screen:** Show the final Pre-Flight score card with persona breakdown.

---

## [2:00 – 2:30] Step 4 — Editor + AI Editing

**Say:**
"Once you know the clip will work, you edit it right here. No switching tools."

**Action:**
1. Show the editor timeline with the selected clip trimmed
2. Type in the AI chat: "Add captions from transcript" → show captions appearing on canvas
3. Briefly show the filter sliders (brightness, saturation)

**Say:**
"The conversational editor compiles plain-English commands directly into editor actions using Gemini's JSON mode. Captions, filters, trim — all driven by natural language."

---

## [2:30 – 2:50] Step 5 — Export

**Say:**
"When it's ready, one click sends it to our Cloud Run render worker — ffmpeg-python processes it server-side and stores the final MP4 in MongoDB GridFS."

**Action:** Click Export → show the export progress indicator.

---

## [2:50 – 3:00] Close

**Say:**
"QuickAI Short: YouTube in, viral short out — validated before you post.
Built with Google ADK, Gemini 2.5 Flash, and Supabase MCP.
Try it live at quickaishort.online."

**Screen:** Homepage or clip result screen with the URL visible.

---

## Recording checklist

- [ ] Use Chrome — COEP/COOP headers require a Chromium browser for Whisper.wasm
- [ ] Log in before recording — editor requires auth
- [ ] Use a real YouTube URL (10–20 min video works best for clip variety)
- [ ] Record at 1920x1080, 60fps if possible
- [ ] Mic: clean audio — no background noise
- [ ] Keep cursor movements deliberate and slow
- [ ] Edit out any network wait > 5 seconds with a cut + "Processing…" overlay
- [ ] Add title card at start: "QuickAI Short — Pre-Flight | Google ADK"
- [ ] Add end card with URL: quickaishort.online
- [ ] Upload to YouTube as unlisted, then add link to Devpost

---

## Backup: if Pre-Flight is slow

If the Pre-Flight pipeline takes > 30s on camera, cut to the result screen and say:
"The full pipeline takes 20–30 seconds as six Gemini agents run in parallel — here's the output."
Then show the result. Don't wait on camera.
